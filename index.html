How to play:

To move from one room to another, type move followed by the direction (North, South, East, West) and press Enter.

The highlighted phrases in the text provide clues about the tasks that need to be done and the means to achieve that. For example, if you find tea spilled on the floor and a bucket with a mop in the corner of the room, type mop floor and press Enter.

Look for clues hidden in the text, ignore articles when typing instructions, and try to remember where in the house you might be able to find relief if you start feeling a bit worn out.
