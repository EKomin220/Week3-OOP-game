Desperate Housewife

A House Cleaning Text Adventure Game

You are a desperate housewife, and your home currently resembles a disaster zone.

Your family has left behind a whirlwind of mess, and it's up to you to restore order. Can you tackle all the chores and return every room to its pristine, perfect state before you lose the will to live?

How to play:

To move from one room to another, type the desired direction (north south, east, west) and press Enter. Look carefully for the hidden clues in the text about the cleaning tasks that need to be completed. When typing your instructions, keep it as brief and ignore articles

So, roll up your sleeves and let's get going!
